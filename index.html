Welcome

HealthDB is a database management system whose data definition language is ADL and whose data manipulation language is AQL. ADL stands for Archetype Definition Language and AQL for Archetype Query Language. ADL and AQL are products of openEHR project.

healdhdb offers a variety of access to the services provided. A client application to issue ADL and AQL statements through a browser and many API drivers for languages like JavaScript, Java, C, C# and Swift.

The design of healthdb was driven by simplicity of use; reliability and performance even in constrained environments.

Simple steps to see it in action

  • Download and unpack the latest version.
  • Run the script healthdb in the bin directory.
  • Open your browser at http://localhost:9876 and enjoy the samples!
